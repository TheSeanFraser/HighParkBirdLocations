This website is intended to provide a useful guide based on the GPS location data of the birds I see in High Park. View the Python source code for this project on GitHub.

Check out the Guide get a brief overview of the "hotspots" around the park!

The GPS locations may not be exact, but they are within a few feet of where the bird was seen. Species with high counts have their locations estimated.

For their protection, vulnerable species (such as owls) will not be shown on the maps.
