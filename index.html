This is where I will publish the data I collect about the GPS locations of the birds I see in High Park.

The GPS locations may not be exact, but they are within a few feet of where the bird was seen. Species with high counts have their locations estimated.

For their protection, vulnerable species will not be shown on the maps.

More detailed species reports will be added next.
